About Me

Hi everyone! My name is Madison Wagers and I recently graduated from Eastern Kentucky University in December of 2022 with my bachelor's degree in elementary education.
Since then, I have been subbing for my local school district, and I am beyond excited to actually get started on my career and have my own classroom when the opportunity arises.
