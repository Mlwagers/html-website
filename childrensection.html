Children's Book Recommendations

Children's Books

Dog Man: Twenty Thousand Fleas Under the Sea
by Dav Pilkey

Piggy has returned, and his newest plot is his most diabolical yet. WHAT other new villains are on the horizon? WHERE are they all coming from? And WHO will step forward to save the city when scoundrels sabotage our Supa Buddies? With themes of friendship and doing good, Dog Man: Twenty Thousand Fleas Under the Sea is packed with action and hilarity. Featuring "Chomp-O-Rama," a brand-new song, a monstrous Mighty Mite -- and so much more than ever before! IT'S HEROIC, IT'S EPIC!


Cinderella--with Dogs!
by Linda Bailey

Here is a Cinderella story like no other. When Cinderella wishes for a fairy godmother, she’s not expecting one that barks! And this fairy dogmother has a different agenda. Yes, Cinderella will have a new dress, but made of a cozy dog blanket. Yes, they will go to the ball, but they will run there and chase squirrels along the way. There’s nothing like a canine companion to get a girl out of the house and her mind off her troubles! Yes, indeed, dogs do make everything better—and are a reminder that you don’t need “happily ever after” when you have the joy of now. Readers will get a kick out of this retelling with its lovable cast of characters and refreshing ending.

The Rainbow Fish Finds His Way Home
by Marcus Pfister

When Rainbow Fish gets lost and separated from his family and friends during an undersea storm, he makes new friends that help him find his way home.

Children's Book Authors

Popular Children's Authors


Laura Numeroff

Laura Numeroff is an American author and illustrator. She is most known for "If You Give A Mouse A Cookie". If you love this book, you will also enjoy her other stories. Some include:
  • If You Give A Pig A Pancake
  • If You Give A Moose A Muffin
  • If You Give A Cat A Cupcake

Eric Carle

Eric Carle was an American author, designer, and illustrator of children's books. Unfortunately, he passed away in 2021. However, his work is not forgotten and he left behind many stories for children to cherish. Some of these include:
  • The Very Hungry Caterpillar
  • The Tiny Seed
  • The Mixed-Up Chameleon

Lucille Colandro

Lucille Colandro is the author who brings the world "There Was An Old Lady" books. If you love these stories, she has so many that she has written over the years, so there is so much fun for you to enjoy! Some include:
  • There Was An Old Lady Who Swallowed a Fly!
  • There Was An Old Lady Who Swallowed a Chick!
  • There Was An Old Lady Who Swallowed a Bat!
