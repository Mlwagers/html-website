Young Adult Book Recommendations

Young Adult Books

The Summer I Turned Pretty
by Jenny Han

Belly spends the summer she turns sixteen at the beach, just like every other summer of her life, but this time, things are very different.

The Inheritance Games
by Jennifer Lynn Barnes

When a Connecticut teenager inherits vast wealth and an eccentric estate from the richest man in Texas, she must also live with his surviving family and solve a series of puzzles to discover how she earned her inheritance.

The Cruel Prince
by Holly Black

Jude, seventeen and mortal, gets tangled in palace intrigues while trying to win a place in the treacherous High Court of Faerie, where she and her sisters have lived for a decade. The story contains profanity, sexual references, and violence.

Young Adult Book Authors


Popular Young Adult's Authors



Suzanne Collins

Suzanne Collins is an American author and television writer. She is most known for "The Hunger Games" series. If you want to dive into a dystopian world, then this series is for you! There is even a new addition to the series that came out in 2020.
  • The Hunger Games
  • Catching Fire
  • Mockingjay
  • The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes

Leigh Bardugo

Leigh Bardugo is an American fantasy author who is best known for her young adult Grishaverse novels. This includes:
  • Shadow and Bone trilogy
  • Six of Crows duology
  • King of Scars duology


Karen M. McManus

Karen M. McManus is an American author. She is most famous for "One Of Us Is Lying", but she has many other books to enjoy. Some of these include:
  • Two Can Keep A Secret
  • One of Us Is Next
  • Nothing More to Tell
  • One of Us Is Back
