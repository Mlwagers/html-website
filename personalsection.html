My Personal Favorites

Here are some books that have stuck with me over the years! I hope you enjoy!

Shatter Me
by Tahereh Mafi

Ostracized her entire life, 17-year-old Juliette is freed from isolation and forced to support a post-apocalyptic dictatorship. Adam, the only person to show her affection, offers hope of a better life. The plot contains profanity and violence.

This is an amazing series to get lost in, and I loved every minute of it!

It Ends With Us
by Colleen Hoover

Combining a captivating romance with a cast of all-too-human characters, It Ends with Us is an unforgettable tale of love that comes at the ultimate price.

I fell in love with these characters, and the story presents a very powerful message!

Divergent
by Veronica Roth

In a future Chicago, 16-year-old Beatrice must choose one faction among five, a decision made more difficult when she discovers that she does not fit into any one group.

I am a huge fan of the dystopian genre, and Veronica Roth has created such an interesting world to get lost in that had my full attention all throughout!

My Summer TBR

Here are some books on my summer TBR! If you have read one of these already, feel free to reach out and let me know what you thought about it!

The Summer of Broken Rules
by K.L Walther

Meredith's family's annual game of assassin at Martha's Vineyard during a summer wedding is the perfect chance to honor her sister's legacy, and finally join the world again. But when she forms an alliance with a cute groomsman, she's at risk of losing both the game … and her heart.

Meet Me at the Lake
by Carley Fortune

A random connection sends two strangers on a daylong adventure where they make a promise one keeps and the other breaks, with life-changing effects, in this breathtaking new novel from the New York Times bestselling author of Every Summer After.

The Unhoneymooners
by Christina Lauren

For two sworn enemies, anything can happen during the Hawaiian trip of a lifetime—maybe even love—in this romantic comedy from the New York Times bestselling authors of Roomies.
